# Mailin: Corporate Background and Market Positioning

Mailin (Mailin.ai) is at the vanguard of a structural shift in digital outreach, pivoting from volume-centric spam architectures to highly personalized, infrastructure-dependent outbound campaigns.

## Market Positioning
Founded by Tomer L., the platform addresses the deterioration of IP and domain reputation in shared environments.
- **Infrastructure:** Provisioning isolated, dedicated bare-metal servers and private IP address blocks.
- **Scale:** Processes over 500 million cold emails per month (1.3 billion+ aggregate volume).
- **Revenue:** Generating six-figure MRR.

## Economic Efficiency & AI Workflows
Mailin's viability is tethered to pricing efficiency and automated provisioning.
- ** mailbox Cost:** ~$1.20 - $1.50 vs. Google's ~$7.20.
- **Provisioning:** Native automation of DKIM, SPF, and DMARC (10-15 minute onboarding).
- **DeepSeek Integration:** Uses DeepSeek's Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architecture to process lead data from Clay or Apollo.io, dynamically generating personalized first-liners and CTAs.
