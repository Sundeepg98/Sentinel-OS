# Mailin: Corporate Background and Market Positioning

The industry has decisivly pivoted from volume-centric, shared-infrastructure spam architectures toward highly personalized outbound campaigns that demand pristine, isolated network environments.

## Market Positioning
Founded by Tomer L. (ex-special forces, Florida), Mailin (Mailin.ai) addresses the deterioration of IP and domain reputation in shared environments.
- **Global Footprint**: Core business in the US; strategic engineering hubs in India (Visakhapatnam, Chennai, Ghaziabad).
- **Visakhapatnam Hub**: Operates under the STPI scheme, providing world-class connectivity and a dense IT talent corridor.
- **Infrastructure Model**: Provisioning completely isolated bare-metal servers and private IP address blocks.
- **Operational Scale**: Processing 500 million cold emails/month; 1.3 billion+ aggregate volume; six-figure MRR.

## Business Model & Economic Efficiency
Mailin automates the historically manual setup of DNS records (DKIM, SPF, DMARC), reducing onboarding to 10-15 minutes.
- **Aggressive Pricing**: ~$1.20 - $1.50 per mailbox vs. Google's ~$7.20 (83% cost reduction).
- **Microservices Optimization**: Profitability is maintained via highly optimized, low-overhead microservices.
- **Non-Profit Verifier**: parses catch-all domains for $14 per 50,000 credits to ensure structural payload validity.

## AI Strategy
Mailin leverages DeepSeek's Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architecture to process raw lead data (from Clay or Apollo.io).
- **Dynamic Generation**: Generates highly personalized first-liners and optimized CTAs.
- **Payload Optimization**: AI optimizes content before injection into dispatch queues to boost engagement and reputation.
